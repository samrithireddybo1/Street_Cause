Task name1: Distribution of groceries

 No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted.We’ve extended our helping hand to the children of Care warriors Foundation by providing groceries as the children here were suffering due to the unaffordability of groceries in this time of crisis. We also spent quality time with the kids by interacting with them while maintaining social distance.

 

Task name2: Project Pragathi

 Health is wealth.We conducted a medical camp in Nidmanoor village of Nalgonda district with the help of 5 doctors. The doctors treated the villagers suffering from joint pains, diabetes, gynaecology problems, and various other skin diseases. Acupuncture and acupressure were carried out simultaneously. Appropriate medicines were given to the villagers and corresponding remedies were suggested by the doctors to ensure the wellbeing of the villagers which in turn would safeguard them from suffering adverse health complications in the future. An immunity medicine was also provided to shield the villagers against coronavirus.
